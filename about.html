About Us

Our Mission


Bringing awareness of the different farmer’s markets located in Prince Georges County

Our Vision


Providing residents in the county with accessible and fresh food

The Farmers Markets in Prince Georges County Maryland website aims to bring visibility to the different Farmers Markets located in the county. The information on the website will allow consumers to find the location of the available farmer’s markets. For the users’ convenience, the platform allows them to see the location of the Market relative to their current location.

Prince Georges’s county demographic is composed of a majority-minority population. Ultimately, this leads to inequality surrounding access to healthy fresh foods. While there are other factors that contribute to access to healthy food such as income and transportation, this website’s objective is to bring awareness of the different resources available in the county.
