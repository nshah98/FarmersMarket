Entering the Website

1. Upon entering the website, users can click on the explore button or navigate between the home, about, services or help tabs.

2. The about and help tabs are stagnant webpages that offer information about the website and its purpose. It is recommended that users navigate to the services tab to locate active information about the different farmers’ markets.

3. To begin using the website, users should click on the “Explore Button” located on the home page or select the services tab to be directed to a directory of the different Farmers Markets located in PG county

The Services Page

1. The services page displays all of the Farmers Markets in Prince George's County along with a map and a table on the left side that displays the names of the market with the farmers’ markets’ associated website.

2. On the services page, you can explore the map to your liking and find a market that interests you. Its recommended that users click on a dropped pin on the map to get detailed information about the market. Clicking on a dropped pin on the map will open up a sidebar on the map that displays the location and the phone number of the market

3. Once you find a market of your liking, you can click the market’s website link supplied in the table to learn more.
